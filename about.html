About Garissa University

Garissa University, located in the heart of northeastern Kenya, is a center of academic excellence and innovation.
We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs that prepare students to solve real-world challenges
through knowledge, research, and community engagement.

Our mission is to provide quality university education that nurtures creativity, professionalism, and ethical leadership. We are proud to be one of the fastest-growing public universities in the region, fostering a peaceful, inclusive, and empowering environment for students from all walks of life.

At Garissa University, we are committed to advancing sustainable development and empowering future leaders to contribute meaningfully to society. Join us as we shape the future through education and innovation.
